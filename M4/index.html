Planet Wars: Conquer the Galaxy


Overview

Planet Wars is an epic interstellar battle game inspired by the iconic Star Wars universe. In this thrilling strategy game, you command your planet, manage your fleet of ships, and control your resources. Your mission is to attack and conquer other planets, gathering essential materials to expand and strengthen your empire.

Gameplay

You start with your planet, complete with its own defense and attack troops. Using your in-game currency, you can build and upgrade your fleet. Each type of troop has a specific cost and unique capabilities. Strategically attacking enemy planets will earn you valuable resources, which can be used to create more powerful ships and defenses.

>

Strategy

To succeed, you'll need to balance your resources between building defensive troops to protect your planet and attack troops to conquer others. Each conquered planet boosts your material reserves, enabling you to enhance your fleet and further your conquest.

Objective

The ultimate goal in Planet Wars is to dominate the galaxy by conquering as many planets as possible. Use your strategic skills and combat prowess to expand your territory and become the supreme ruler of the stars.
